Biography

Elizabeth Schulz, M.Sc. graduated from PUC-RJ (Pontificia Universidade Católica do Rio de Janeiro) in 1983. She received her Master’s degree from Getúlio Vargas University-RJ, in 1986.

She moved to Quebec, Canada and was accepted as a member at the Quebec Order of Psychologists in 1992 when she started her private practice in Montreal.

She also graduated in social work from McGill University in 1997, and worked as a social worker at a CLSC NDG/MTL O (Local Community Services Center Notre-Dame-de-Grâce/Montréal Ouest) up to 2003.

From 2003 to March 2015, she worked as a psychologist at Richardson Hospital, CSSS Cavendish.

Elizabeth completed her training in Hypnotherapy Ericksonienne at the Centre de Formation et Consultation en Hypnose du Québec in 2011.

Presently, she works with adolescents, adults, couples and families in her private practice. She offers treatment for anxiety, depression, loss or grief, post-traumatic stress disorder, relationship issues, chronic pain or illness and more.

Elizabeth Schulz offers counseling through Skype, for clients outside Montreal.
