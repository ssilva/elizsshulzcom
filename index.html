Biography

Elizabeth came from Brazil, where she graduated in clinical psychology at PUC-RJ. She became a member of the Quebec Order of Psychologists in 1992.

Elizabeth believes in our great potential to overcome difficulties and that these are great opportunities for personal growth and progress.

Elizabeth Schulz has extensive experience in clinical psychology and in working with anxiety, depression, grief, interpersonal and family relationships, adaptation difficulties, divorce, burnout, etc. She works with the ACT (Acceptance and Commitment Therapy) approach in Cognitive and Behavioral Therapy.

Languages: Portuguese, French and English.
Attendance only by videoconference.
